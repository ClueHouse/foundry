Our Story

A Hole in One was created to feel like a tiny tropical holiday hiding in the middle of Tauranga.

We wanted a course that children could enjoy immediately, adults would still find challenging, and families would talk about on the drive home.

Every corner is designed for laughter, friendly rivalry and one more round.
