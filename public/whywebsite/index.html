Why have a website?

Because people expect one.

When someone hears about your business, they’ll Google you. If you’re not there, they move on.

A website gives them a solid place to land: it shows you’re established, answers the usual questions, and makes it easy to get in touch. And for roughly the cost of a single newspaper ad, you get something that doesn’t vanish tomorrow.

It just gets on with the job while you get on with yours.
